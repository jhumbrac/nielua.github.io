A story about self reflection and self-discovery.

Hello my name is Mifek Nielua and I am am the author of the Broke Me (free) Trilogy and its companion novels, and I’m one of the producers of the movie adaptations. I am thrilled to announce that my new book, Give to Give, Not to Get, will be published on 14 July 2020. A Princess Mononoke story for the 21st century, it’s a passionate strive for strength and humility set in Palau, Micronesia. Please explore the book’s page for more details. On this site, you’ll find information about all my books and links to all my social media. Please follow and come say hi! Enjoy! In an effort to heal oneself, honing the ability to forgive
and embark on a path of accountability and introspection one must look inward.
